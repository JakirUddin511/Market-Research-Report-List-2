Vertical metal detectors are optimized for specific product flows, often installed above conveyor belts to detect contaminants in bulk materials. Horizontal metal detectors, on the other hand, are designed for products moving horizontally, allowing for easy integration in diverse processing setups. Both types ensure product safety by identifying metal contaminants during various stages of food production.</p></p>
