<p><p>Fluorocarbon barrier films are categorized into three main types: PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene), PVDF (Polyvinylidene Fluoride), and PEVE (Polyethylene Vinyl Ether). PTFE offers exceptional chemical resistance and low friction properties, making it suitable for high-performance applications. PVDF is known for its durability and good thermal stability, often used in coatings and membranes. PEVE combines flexibility with chemical resistance, making it ideal for diverse industrial uses. Together, these materials cater to various applications, enhancing product performance and longevity.</p></p>
