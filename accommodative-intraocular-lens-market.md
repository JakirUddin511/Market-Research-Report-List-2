<p><strong>The Accommodative Intraocular Lens Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Optical Surface</li><li>Double Optical Surface</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The accommodative intraocular lens (IOL) market is primarily divided into two types: single optical surface and double optical surface lenses. Single optical surface lenses feature a single curvature to allow for some degree of accommodation, providing limited focus adjustment. In contrast, double optical surface lenses incorporate two curvatures, enhancing the eye's ability to focus at various distances. Both types aim to improve vision post-cataract surgery, offering patients a wider range of clarity and reducing dependence on glasses.</p></p>
