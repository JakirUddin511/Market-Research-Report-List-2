<p><p>PCI Express (PCIe) market types refer to different generations of the PCIe standard, which defines high-speed computer expansion bus standards. Gen1 offers a maximum data rate of 2.5 GT/s per lane, suitable for basic connectivity. Gen2 doubles this speed to 5 GT/s, enhancing performance for data-intensive applications. Gen3 further increases the throughput to 8 GT/s, enabling faster data transfer rates for advanced technologies like SSDs and high-performance graphics cards. Each generation caters to evolving demands in computing performance and efficiency.</p></p>
