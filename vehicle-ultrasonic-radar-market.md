Forward Radar is designed to detect obstacles in the vehicle's path while moving forward, aiding in adaptive cruise control and collision avoidance systems. In contrast, Reversing Radar focuses on monitoring the area behind the vehicle during reverse maneuvers, enhancing safety by alerting drivers to nearby objects. Both types enhance vehicle safety, assisting drivers in navigating complex environments and reducing the risk of accidents.</p></p>
