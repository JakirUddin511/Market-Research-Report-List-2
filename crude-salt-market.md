<p><p>The crude salt market comprises several types: </p><p>1. **Sea Salt** is harvested through evaporation of seawater, favored for its minerals.</p><p>2. **Well Salt** is extracted from subterranean brine through drilling.</p><p>3. **Lake Salt** comes from evaporated saline lakes, often rich in diverse minerals.</p><p>4. **Rock Salt**, or halite, is mined from underground deposits.</p><p>5. **Virgin Salt** refers to unrefined or minimally processed salt.</p><p>6. **Regenerated Salt** is produced by processing salt brine, often reutilizing waste. </p><p>Each type serves distinct industrial and culinary applications.</p></p>
