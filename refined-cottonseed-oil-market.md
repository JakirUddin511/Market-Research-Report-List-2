<p><p>The refined cottonseed oil market encompasses two primary types: crude cottonseed oil and edible cottonseed oil. Crude cottonseed oil is the unrefined form, extracted directly from cotton seeds, often used in industrial applications or as a base for further refining. Edible cottonseed oil, on the other hand, undergoes rigorous refining processes to eliminate impurities and ensure safety for consumption. It is commonly used in cooking and food production due to its neutral flavor and high smoke point, appealing to health-conscious consumers.</p></p>
