<p><p>The etching metal product market includes two primary types: wet etching and dry etching. Wet etching utilizes liquid chemicals to remove material from a substrate, offering advantages like uniformity and high selectivity, making it ideal for large surfaces. In contrast, dry etching employs gases or plasmas to achieve precise etching on smaller scales, allowing for intricate designs and higher control over the etching process. Both methods are essential in various industries, including electronics, automotive, and aerospace, for fabricating components.</p></p>
