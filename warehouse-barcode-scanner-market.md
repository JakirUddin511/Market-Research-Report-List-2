<p><p>The warehouse barcode scanner market can be segmented into handheld and fixed barcode scanners. Handheld warehouse barcode scanners are portable devices that enable workers to easily scan barcodes while moving through the warehouse, enhancing flexibility and efficiency. Conversely, fixed warehouse barcode scanners are installed at specific locations, such as conveyor belts or entry points, providing automated scanning of items as they pass by. Both types serve to streamline inventory management and improve accuracy in tracking goods within the warehouse environment.</p></p>
