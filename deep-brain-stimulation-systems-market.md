<p><p>Deep Brain Stimulation (DBS) Systems are medical devices designed to deliver electrical impulses to specific brain regions, aiding in the treatment of neurological disorders such as Parkinson's disease, essential tremors, and dystonia. <p><p>The Deep Brain Stimulation (DBS) systems market is primarily segmented into single-channel and dual-channel deep brain stimulators. Single-channel DBS systems deliver electrical impulses through one lead to target specific brain regions, often used for conditions like Parkinson's disease. In contrast, dual-channel DBS systems utilize two leads, allowing for more complex stimulation patterns and broader treatment capabilities, which may improve outcomes for patients with multifaceted neurological disorders. The choice between these systems depends on the specific clinical needs and treatment goals.</p></p>
