<p><p>The Industrial Electrodialysis Bipolar Membrane System market is characterized by two primary types: Continuous Electrodialysis Systems and Batch Electrodialysis Systems. Continuous systems facilitate a constant flow of feed solution, allowing for uninterrupted ion separation and production, making them suitable for large-scale applications. Conversely, Batch Electrodialysis Systems process discrete batches of feed, offering flexibility for varied operational scales and specific applications. Both types leverage electrostatic principles to efficiently separate ions, driving advancements in desalination, wastewater treatment, and resource recovery.</p></p>
