<p><p>The Magnetically Controlled Capsule Gastroscopy market encompasses various technologies for gastrointestinal imaging. Electromagnets offer dynamic control and precise navigation of the capsule within the digestive tract, allowing for real-time imaging. Permanent magnets provide a simpler, more cost-effective solution for positioning the capsule but may lack the flexibility of electromagnets. Magnetic Resonance imaging enhances the visual quality of the images obtained, leveraging magnetic fields for detailed soft tissue visualization, contributing to comprehensive gastrointestinal diagnostics and treatment planning.</p></p>
