<p><p>Healthcare and medical analytics can be categorized into three main types: descriptive, predictive, and prescriptive. Descriptive analytics focuses on summarizing historical data to identify trends and patterns, helping healthcare professionals understand past performance. Predictive analytics utilizes statistical techniques and machine learning to forecast future events based on historical data. Prescriptive analytics goes a step further by recommending actions based on predicted outcomes, enabling healthcare organizations to optimize decision-making and resources for improved patient care and operational efficiency.</p></p>
