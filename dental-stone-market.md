<p><ul><li>Model Dental Stone</li><li>Die Dental Stone</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental stone market is primarily divided into two main types: model dental stone and die dental stone. Model dental stone is utilized for creating dental models, providing a durable and precise representation of patient anatomy, often used for orthodontics and prosthodontics. In contrast, die dental stone is formulated for fabricating detailed dental restorations, offering superior hardness and accuracy for producing precise tooth replicas. Both types are essential in the dental industry for ensuring high-quality patient care and treatment outcomes.</p></p>
