<p><p>The IoT RFID scanners market is primarily divided into two types: fixed and handheld scanners. Fixed RFID scanners are stationary devices installed at strategic locations, such as doorways or conveyor belts, to automatically read RFID tags on moving items, enhancing inventory management and asset tracking. On the other hand, handheld scanners are portable devices designed for mobility, allowing users to scan RFID tags in various settings, making them ideal for tasks like stock-taking and maintenance checks in diverse environments.</p></p>
