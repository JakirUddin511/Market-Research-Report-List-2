<p><p>The Charging Interface Control Unit (CICU) market consists of two primary types: DC Control Units and AC Control Units. DC Control Units facilitate direct current charging, enabling rapid charging for electric vehicles, promoting efficiency and reduced charging times. In contrast, AC Control Units support alternating current charging, commonly used in residential and slower public charging stations. Both types play a crucial role in standardizing communication between electric vehicles and charging infrastructure, enhancing overall user experience and charging convenience.</p></p>
