<p><p>Pharmaceutical warehousing encompasses two primary market types: non-cold chain and cold chain warehouses. Non-cold chain warehouses store standard medications and products that do not require temperature control, ensuring safe storage at room temperature. In contrast, cold chain warehouses provide temperature-sensitive storage for pharmaceuticals, such as vaccines and biologics, which need precise temperature management to maintain efficacy. Both types play crucial roles in the pharmaceutical supply chain, ensuring the safe and effective distribution of healthcare products.</p></p>
