<p><strong>The OPP Release Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Siliceous Type"</li><li>"Non-Silicon Type"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The OPP release film market is divided into two primary types: Siliceous and Non-Silicon. Siliceous type release films utilize silica-based coatings that offer excellent release properties and high-temperature resistance, making them ideal for various applications like laminating and adhesive release. On the other hand, Non-Silicon type release films employ alternative coatings, such as fluoropolymers or acrylates, providing good release characteristics but often at lower temperature thresholds. Each type caters to specific industry needs and performance requirements.</p></p>
