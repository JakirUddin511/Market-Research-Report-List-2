<p><ul><li>Ionized Breast Imaging Device</li><li>Non Ionized Breast Imaging Device</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The breast imaging devices market is divided into two main categories: ionized and non-ionized imaging devices. Ionized breast imaging devices, such as mammography, utilize ionizing radiation to create detailed images of breast tissue for cancer detection. Non-ionized breast imaging devices, including ultrasound and magnetic resonance imaging (MRI), use sound waves or magnetic fields instead of radiation, making them safer alternatives. Both types play crucial roles in diagnosing breast conditions, enhancing patient outcomes through early detection and monitoring.</p></p>
