<p><ul><li>Single Power Supply</li><li>Dual Power Supply</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-speed amplifiers market can be categorized into single power supply and dual power supply types. Single power supply amplifiers operate with a single voltage source, simplifying design and integration, making them ideal for low-power applications. In contrast, dual power supply amplifiers utilize both positive and negative voltage sources, offering enhanced performance and linearity for high-frequency or high-dynamic range applications. These distinctions impact their usage across various sectors, including telecommunications, automotive, and consumer electronics, driven by specific performance requirements.</p></p>
