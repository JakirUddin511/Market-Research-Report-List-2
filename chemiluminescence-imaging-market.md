<p><strong>The Chemiluminescence Imaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chemical for Chemiluminescence</li><li>Biochemical for Bioluminescence</li><li>Electromagnetic as Electroluminescence</li><li>Photons as Photoluminescence</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Chemiluminescence Imaging market encompasses various types based on the mechanisms of light emission. Chemical chemiluminescence involves reactions that emit light without external excitation, often utilized in assays. Biochemical luminescence, driven by biological processes, is prominent in imaging applications like reporter gene assays. Electroluminescence involves light emission through electrical excitation, found in devices like LEDs. Photoluminescence centers around the absorption and re-emission of photons, widely applied in fluorescence imaging and detection technologies. Each type serves distinct applications across research and diagnostics.</p></p>
