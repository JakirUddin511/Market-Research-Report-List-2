<p><p>The four-way ball valves market includes various types, primarily focusing on manual and pneumatic variants. Manual four-way ball valves rely on physical operation for control, offering simplicity and reliability for smaller applications. In contrast, pneumatic four-way ball valves utilize compressed air for automated control, enhancing efficiency and precision, particularly in larger or industrial setups. Both types serve essential roles in fluid control systems, with manual types suitable for localized use and pneumatic types suited for automated processes in complex systems.</p></p>
