<p><p>The plastic zipper market is categorized into three main types: close-end zippers, open-end zippers, and two-way zippers. Close-end zippers are typically used in items like bags and jackets, providing a secure closure without the ability to open from both ends. Open-end zippers allow two-way access and are commonly found in items such as jackets and tents. Two-way zippers feature sliders at both ends, enabling versatile openings, making them ideal for luggage and specialized apparel. Each type serves distinct functional requirements in various applications.</p></p>
