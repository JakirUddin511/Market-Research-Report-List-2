<p><p>The railway brake pads market comprises two primary types: composite brake pads and powder metallurgy brake pads. Composite brake pads are made from a blend of materials, offering lightweight, high-performance braking with reduced noise and wear. They are typically used in various train models for their versatility and effectiveness. Powder metallurgy brake pads, on the other hand, are crafted from metal powders, providing excellent durability and heat resistance. They are favored for heavy-duty applications due to their strength and longevity under extreme conditions.</p></p>
