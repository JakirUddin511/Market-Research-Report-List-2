<p><p>The automotive heat shields market is segmented into three key types: rigid heat shields, flexible heat shields, and textile heat shields. Rigid heat shields are made from durable materials like metal and provide solid protection against high temperatures, often used in critical areas like the exhaust system. Flexible heat shields, typically constructed from materials like fiberglass or composites, can conform to various shapes, providing effective heat insulation in tight spaces. Textile heat shields utilize advanced fabrics for lightweight yet effective thermal protection, suitable for a range of automotive applications.</p></p>
