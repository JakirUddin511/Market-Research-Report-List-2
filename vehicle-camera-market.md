<p><ul><li>Viewing Camera</li><li>Sensing Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vehicle camera market can be categorized into two main types: viewing cameras and sensing cameras. Viewing cameras primarily focus on providing clear visual feedback to drivers, enhancing visibility and situational awareness during maneuvers such as parking or lane changes. In contrast, sensing cameras are integrated into advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS) to collect data for features like object detection, lane-keeping assistance, and collision avoidance. Both types play crucial roles in improving vehicle safety and enhancing the overall driving experience.</p></p>
