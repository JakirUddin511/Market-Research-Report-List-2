<p><ul><li>Plate Type</li><li>Pellicle Type</li><li>Cube Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The beam splitters market is categorized into three main types: plate type, pellicle type, and cube type. Plate type beam splitters consist of a thin, flat glass plate coated to allow partial transmission and reflection of light. Pellicle type beam splitters are ultra-thin, minimizing optical distortion and making them ideal for high-resolution applications. Cube type beam splitters are compact and combine two right-angle prisms with a thin film coating, providing durable performance in various optical setups, especially in laboratory settings.</p></p>
