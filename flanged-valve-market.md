<p><p>The flanged valve market encompasses various types, each serving specific applications. Ball valves provide quick shut-off with minimal flow resistance, ideal for on/off control. Globe valves excel in regulating flow and pressure, making them suitable for fine adjustments. Butterfly valves are compact and lightweight, often used in large-diameter piping systems for efficient flow control. Gate valves are designed for full flow and are typically used in isolation applications. Other valves include check and safety valves, which serve unique requirements across industries.</p></p>
