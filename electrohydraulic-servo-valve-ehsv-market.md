<p><p>The Electrohydraulic Servo Valve (EHSV) market comprises various types of valves, each serving specific applications. Nozzle flapper valves control hydraulic fluid flow using a flapper that pivots to direct fluid from nozzles. Jet action valves utilize a jet of fluid to create a force that adjusts flow, providing precise control. Dynamic valves feature a flexible design that can adapt to changing conditions, enhancing performance. Together, these valve types contribute to the efficiency and precision of hydraulic systems across industries.</p></p>
