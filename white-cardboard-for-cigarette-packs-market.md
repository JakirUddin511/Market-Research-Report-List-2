<p><p>The White Cardboard for Cigarette Packs market primarily consists of two types: Solid Bleached Sulfate (SBS) and Folding Boxboard (FBB). SBS is a premium quality paperboard made from bleached virgin wood fibers, offering excellent printability and a smooth surface, ideal for high-end packaging. In contrast, FBB is a multi-layer board made from a mix of mechanical and virgin fibers, providing good strength and print quality at a lower cost, making it suitable for economy cigarette packaging.</p></p>
